Lyve - Privacy Policy
Operated by TevDed (Teddy) · Contact: tevded@gmail.com
Effective date: June 28, 2026

Lyve is a browser extension that adds a time-synced chat experience to YouTube videos. This policy explains what data Lyve collects, how it is used, and your choices. By creating an account or posting messages in Lyve, you agree to this policy.

1. Who operates Lyve
Lyve is developed and published by TevDed. For any privacy questions or data requests, contact tevded@gmail.com.

2. Data we collect
Email address - used to create and sign in to your Lyve account.
Password - entered to authenticate. It is transmitted directly to Google Firebase Authentication and is not stored or readable by the extension.
Google account information - if you choose "Sign in with Google," we receive your name and email address from Google to create or sign in to your Lyve account.
YouTube channel information - if you choose to verify channel ownership, Lyve reads your YouTube channel ID and name (with your permission, via Google) solely to confirm you own the channel. We do not access your videos, watch history, or other YouTube data.
Profile - your display name, username, and cosmetic chat appearance (username color and profile banner color), shown next to your messages.
Chat messages - the text you post, along with the YouTube video ID and the video timestamp the message is anchored to.
Channel moderation data - if you own or moderate a channel, moderator assignments, chat settings (e.g. slow mode, emote-only), and any bans/timeouts you set are stored so your channel's chat rules can be enforced.
Public YouTube comments - Lyve reads public comments on the video you are watching (via the YouTube Data API) to surface ones that reference a timestamp; these are shown on the timeline, marked as from YouTube.
Authentication tokens - issued by Firebase and stored locally on your device (via chrome.storage.local) to keep you signed in.
Local preferences - chat settings (e.g. display name, colors, layout options) stored locally on your device.
Lyve does not collect your browsing history, location, financial information, health information, or the content of web pages other than the YouTube video ID needed to attach your chat messages to a video.

3. How we use data
To authenticate your account (email/password or Google) and keep you signed in.
To store chat messages and display them to other users watching the same video.
To display public, timestamped comments from the video on the timeline, read through the YouTube Data API and marked as coming from YouTube.
To verify YouTube channel ownership for creators who opt in, and to enable channel-scoped moderation and chat controls (moderators, bans/timeouts, slow mode, emote-only).
To manage access during the private beta (an approved-users allowlist).
To remember your local chat preferences.
4. Where data is stored and processed
Account and message data are stored using Google Firebase (Authentication and Cloud Firestore) and processed by Google Cloud Functions, running on Google Cloud infrastructure. Authentication tokens and preferences are stored locally in your browser. All network communication uses HTTPS.

5. Third-party services and disclosure
We do not sell your data, and we do not share it with third parties for advertising. Data is processed by:

Google (Firebase / Google Cloud / YouTube Data API) - our hosting, authentication, and backend provider, and the source of public timestamped comments.
7TV and BetterTTV - if emotes are enabled, your browser loads emote images directly from these public emote CDNs. As with loading any image, those services receive your IP address and the requested emote. Lyve does not send them your account information.
Chat messages and your display name/appearance are visible to other Lyve users viewing the same video, which is the core function of the product. We do not use or transfer your data for any purpose unrelated to Lyve, and we do not use it to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes.

6. Data retention and deletion
Your account and messages are retained until you request deletion. To delete your account and associated data, email tevded@gmail.com and we will remove your account and messages from our systems.

7. Children
Lyve is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact tevded@gmail.com and we will delete it.

8. Security
We rely on Google Firebase's security infrastructure, transmit all data over HTTPS, and store authentication tokens locally on your device. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

9. Relationship to YouTube
Lyve is an independent third-party extension. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by YouTube or Google LLC.

10. Changes to this policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
