# Lyve - Privacy Policy

Operated by TevDed (Teddy) · Contact: tevded@gmail.com
Effective date: June 23, 2026

Lyve is a browser extension that adds a time-synced chat experience to YouTube videos. This policy explains what data Lyve collects, how it is used, and your choices. By creating an account or posting messages in Lyve, you agree to this policy.

## 1. Who operates Lyve
Lyve is developed and published by **TevDed**. For any privacy questions or data requests, contact tevded@gmail.com.

## 2. Data we collect
- **Email address** - used to create and sign in to your Lyve account.
- **Password** - entered to authenticate. It is transmitted directly to Google Firebase Authentication and is *not* stored or readable by the extension.
- **Google account information** - if you choose "Sign in with Google," we receive your name and email address from Google to create or sign in to your Lyve account.
- **YouTube channel information** - if you choose to verify channel ownership, Lyve reads your YouTube channel ID and name (with your permission, via Google) solely to confirm that you own the channel. We do not access your videos, watch history, or other YouTube data.
- **Display name / username** - shown next to your messages.
- **Chat messages** - the text you post, along with the YouTube video ID and the video timestamp the message is anchored to.
- **Authentication tokens** - issued by Firebase and stored locally on your device to keep you signed in.
- **Local preferences** - chat settings (display name, colors, layout) stored locally on your device.

Lyve does **not** collect your browsing history, location, financial information, or YouTube data beyond what is described above.

## 3. How we use data
- To authenticate your account (email/password or Google) and keep you signed in.
- To store chat messages and display them to other users watching the same video.
- To verify YouTube channel ownership for creators who opt in, and to enable channel-scoped moderation.
- To manage access during the private beta (an approved-users allowlist).
- To remember your local chat preferences.

## 4. Where data is stored and processed
Account and message data are stored using **Google Firebase** (Authentication and Cloud Firestore) and processed by **Google Cloud Functions**, running on Google Cloud infrastructure. Authentication tokens and preferences are stored locally in your browser. All network communication uses HTTPS.

## 5. Sharing and disclosure
We do **not** sell your data, and we do not share it with third parties for advertising. Data is processed only by Google (Firebase / Google Cloud) as our hosting, authentication, and backend provider. Chat messages and your display name are visible to other Lyve users viewing the same video, which is the core function of the product. We do not use or transfer your data for any purpose unrelated to Lyve, or to determine creditworthiness or for lending.

## 6. Data retention and deletion
Your account and messages are retained until you request deletion. To delete your account and associated data, email tevded@gmail.com and we will remove your account and messages from our systems.

## 7. Children
Lyve is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact tevded@gmail.com and we will delete it.

## 8. Security
We rely on Google Firebase's and Google Cloud's security infrastructure, transmit all data over HTTPS, and store authentication tokens locally on your device. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

## 9. Relationship to YouTube
Lyve is an independent third-party extension. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by YouTube or Google LLC.

## 10. Changes to this policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
